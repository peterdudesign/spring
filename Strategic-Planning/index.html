Strategic plan 2025 - 2029

At Toronto Community Housing Corporation, we are dedicated to more than just providing homes. We are committed to building vibrant, sustainable communities where residents can thrive and prosper.

Find out more about our strategic plan to nurture thriving communities and help us achieve our vision of transforming our housing community in Toronto for a brighter future.
